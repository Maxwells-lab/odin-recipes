JOLLOF RICE -GHANA JOLLOF

Ghana Jollof

This is an African taste -Ghana Jollof . Loved by the local and foreign people

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Green Pepper

STEPS

  1. Set oil
  2. Add your Chopped vegetables
  3. Wait until it simmers
  4. Add tomatoes paste and stir
  5. Wait for some time and add your rice
  6. Add water and stir
